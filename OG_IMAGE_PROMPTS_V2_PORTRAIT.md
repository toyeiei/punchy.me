## Midjourney Pro Tips

### For Best Results:
1. **Use `--style raw`** for photorealistic cinematic quality
2. **Use `--ar 16:9`** for correct aspect ratio (will give you 1920x1080 at high resolution)
3. **Use `--v 6`** for latest Midjourney model
4. **Add camera specs** (RED, ARRI, Sony Venice) for cinematic look
5. **Emphasize "looking directly at camera"** for eye contact
6. **Use "close-up" or "medium close-up"** for portrait framing

### Generation Workflow:
```
1. Paste full prompt in Midjourney Discord
2. Wait for 4 variations to generate
3. Choose best one (U1, U2, U3, or U4 to upscale)
4. Download high-resolution image
5. Crop/resize to exactly 1920x1080 if needed
6. Convert to WebP format
```

### Post-Processing (Optional):
- **Brightness**: Ensure faces are well-lit but maintain dark background
- **Contrast**: Boost for dramatic cinematic look
- **Saturation**: Slightly increase neon green accents
- **Sharpening**: Add subtle sharpening for ultra-crisp details
